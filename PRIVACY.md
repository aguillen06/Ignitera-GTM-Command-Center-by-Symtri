# Privacy Policy for Ignitera GTM Command Center

**Last Updated:** December 2, 2025

## Introduction

Ignitera GTM Command Center ("we," "our," or "the Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you use Ignitera GTM Command Center, we may collect:

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and authentication credentials
- **CRM Data**: Lead information, company data, contact details, and sales activities you enter
- **Voice Data**: Audio recordings and transcripts when using the voice assistant feature (processed in real-time, not permanently stored)
- **Usage Data**: Application interaction patterns, feature usage, and session information

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, access times, and pages viewed
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
- **Cookies**: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Enable core CRM, lead management, and AI enrichment features
2. **AI Processing**: Use Google Gemini AI to generate insights, enrichment data, and outbound content
3. **Improve Application**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
4. **Communication**: Send service-related notifications and updates
5. **Security**: Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

## Third-Party Services

### Google Gemini AI
We use Google's Gemini AI API to provide:
- Lead enrichment and analysis
- ICP generation
- Outbound content creation
- Voice assistant functionality

Data sent to Gemini is processed according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Supabase
We use Supabase for:
- Database storage
- User authentication
- Real-time data synchronization

Data is stored according to [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

## Data Storage and Security

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
- **Data Retention**: We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
- **Backups**: Regular backups are performed to prevent data loss

2. **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data
4. **Export**: Export your data in a portable format
5. **Opt-Out**: Disable specific features (e.g., voice assistant)

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing
